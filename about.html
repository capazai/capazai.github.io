About Capaz AI

Making AI accessible for everyone

The name says it all

"Capaz" is Portuguese for "capable." That's our entire mission in one word: to make you capable of using AI tools with confidence.

Too many people feel left behind by the rapid pace of technology. They hear about AI on the news, see their competitors using it, but don't know where to start. The tutorials are confusing, the terminology is intimidating, and the "help" often makes things worse.

We believe artificial intelligence shouldn't be intimidating or exclusive. Everyone deserves access to these tools—not just tech-savvy early adopters.

Why this matters to me

I've spent years working with technology and have seen firsthand how transformative these tools can be—when people actually know how to use them.

Too often, I've watched smart, capable people struggle because they were taught by someone who couldn't explain things clearly. Someone who used jargon without realizing it. Someone who moved too fast.

I started Capaz AI because I believe patience is the missing ingredient in most tech education. Whether you're a retiree who wants to stay sharp, a small business owner looking for an edge, or anyone in between—you deserve someone who will take the time to make sure you truly understand.

What I believe

Patience isn't optional

Everyone learns at their own pace. I never rush, never judge, and always take the time to make sure you truly understand.

Plain language only

I explain things in terms you can understand. If something doesn't make sense, that's on me—not you.

Results over theory

I don't waste time on theory you won't use. Everything we cover is something you can apply right away.

Honesty first

If AI isn't the right solution for your problem, I'll tell you. I'm here to help, not to sell you something you don't need.

Let's talk

I'd love to hear about your goals and see how I can help. Book a free call—no pressure, no obligation.
